Flashcard

A flashcard has a cue or hint on the front side, and a corresponding answer on the back side. The cue can be a question or just one word that prompts or triggers an anticipated response. Anything that can be studied in a “question and answer” format can be turned into flashcards — from definitions, foreign language vocabularies, scientific symbols, historical dates and traffic signs, to countries and their respective capitals or currencies.
